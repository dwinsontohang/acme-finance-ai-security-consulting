# Statement of Work

## Problem Statement

A clear understanding of the core challenges is essential to ensure ZKC Consulting addresses the right security priorities in ACME Finance's AI strategy. By clearly articulating the core issues, we can help the CTO and CISO teams steer AI innovation without compromising on security, compliance, or operational resilience.

### CTO Team's Problem Statement

- ACME Finance lacks unified technical and procedural security controls tailored to its AI products—RAG-enabled chatbots, AI Co-pilots, and Agentic AI—resulting in inconsistent protection across the enterprise.
- The adoption of these AI systems is expanding the internal and external attack surface in ways that remain poorly understood and unaddressed, raising the risk of misuse, data exposure, and external threats.
- The growing integration of AI into core operations presents unresolved challenges around compliance, data governance, and accountability, potentially undermining regulatory readiness and organisational trust.

### CISO Team's Problem Statement

- The Security Operations team is moving from LogRhythm to Azure Sentinel, but lacks clear guidance on how to effectively integrate Azure Security Copilot and SOAR capabilities into daily operations.
- Security analysts need practical support to apply Copilot's features for incident response, vulnerability management, and threat intelligence without disrupting established workflows.
- Without a structured approach, there is a risk of misusing or underutilising AI tools, leading to reduced detection quality, inconsistent automation, and governance gaps.
