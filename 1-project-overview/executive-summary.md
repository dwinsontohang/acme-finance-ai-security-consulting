# Executive Summary

## Purpose of the Engagement
ACME Finance engaged ZKC Consulting to ensure the secure, compliant, and scalable adoption of AI across its global enterprise. This initiative was driven by the executive mandate to modernise operations using AI, while maintaining strong data protection, operational integrity, and alignment with regulatory obligations such as GDPR.

## Scope of Work
The engagement focused on two key workstreams: the CTO team, responsible for AI architecture strategy and deployment, and the CISO team, leading the secure integration of AI within security operations. ZKC Consulting supported AI product rollout and security tooling modernisation through Azure Sentinel and Security Copilot.

## Strategic Deliverables
ZKC delivered a suite of outcomes including an AI system security architecture blueprint, a monitoring and incident response framework, risk assessments, governance and adoption guidance, and role-based training programmes. Each deliverable was aligned to business objectives and designed to support long-term sustainability and security maturity.

## Delivery Methodology
Both workstreams were delivered through a structured 3-month sprint-based plan. Each sprint aligned with a defined set of technical and non-technical outputs, and followed ZKC's 4D framework—Diagnose, Design, Deliver, and Defend—to ensure a clear, accountable, and phased rollout across stakeholders and systems.

## Risk & Compliance Alignment
Throughout the engagement, ZKC placed particular emphasis on managing risks related to data privacy, access control, and regulatory compliance. A unified RAID log was maintained to track risks, assumptions, issues, and dependencies, with targeted focus on GDPR compliance, data residency, and secure automation.

## Results and Outcomes
The outcome of this programme is a future-ready, secure AI foundation for ACME Finance. Both architecture and operations are now equipped with clearly defined security boundaries, operational playbooks, and scalable monitoring. The project was completed on time, with transparency in cost, and clarity in ownership.
