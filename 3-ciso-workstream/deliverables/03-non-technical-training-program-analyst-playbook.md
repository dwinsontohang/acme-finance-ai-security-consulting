# Training Program & Analyst Playbook

**Deliverable Type:** Non-Technical  
**Status:** Final

## Overview
To prepare SOC analysts for AI integration, ZKC Consulting has developed a focused training programme and a supporting playbook. The materials are tailored to real-world use of Copilot and Sentinel in daily operations.

## Included Elements
- Role-based training aligned with incident response and threat intelligence tasks
- Scenario-driven playbook with actionable guidance and escalation procedures
- Supplementary content for ongoing learning and analyst enablement

## Outcome
This deliverable equips teams to confidently and securely use AI within existing security workflows.
