# Security Training Program & Materials

**Deliverable Type:** Non-Technical  
**Status:** Final

## Overview
ZKC Consulting has developed targeted training to support responsible AI adoption across ACME Finance's stakeholder groups.

## Program Components
- **Role-specific sessions** for technical teams, business users, and leadership
- **Practical guidance** on secure AI use, risk handling, and escalation (NIST, 2023)
- **Supporting materials** for ongoing reinforcement, including job aids and quick-reference guides (ISO, 2022)

## Outcome
This training strengthens secure AI practices, improves awareness, and supports consistent policy alignment across the organisation.
