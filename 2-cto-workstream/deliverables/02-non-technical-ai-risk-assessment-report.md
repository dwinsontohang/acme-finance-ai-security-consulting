# AI Risk Assessment Report

**Deliverable Type:** Non-Technical  
**Status:** Final

## Overview
ZKC Consulting has delivered a focused assessment to help ACME Finance understand and manage security risks across its AI products—RAG-enabled chatbots, AI Co-pilots, and Agentic AI systems.

## Key Contents
- **Product-specific threat scenarios** with segmented risk profiles (MITRE, 2023)
- **Likelihood and impact ratings** to support prioritization (ISO, 2018)
- **Mitigation actions** aligned with internal policy and regulatory standards (ICO, 2022)

## Outcome
This assessment enables informed decisions on risk reduction while supporting secure, scalable AI adoption.
